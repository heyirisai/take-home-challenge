REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Document Intelligence & Automation Platform
Issued by: Acme Financial Services

RFP Reference: ACME-2024-DOC-001
Date Issued: November 1, 2024
Response Deadline: December 1, 2024

---

SECTION 1: COMPANY BACKGROUND

Acme Financial Services is a mid-size financial services firm with 500 employees across 3 offices. We process approximately 10,000 documents monthly including loan applications, compliance reports, and client correspondence. We are seeking a document intelligence platform to modernize our document workflows.

---

SECTION 2: QUESTIONS

Please provide detailed responses to each question below.

Question 1: Company Overview
Please provide an overview of your company, including founding date, size, funding status, and relevant experience in the financial services sector.

Question 2: Document Processing Capabilities
What document formats does your platform support? Please describe your ingestion pipeline, including OCR capabilities, processing speed, and any file size limitations.

Question 3: Security & Compliance
Describe your security certifications and compliance posture. Specifically address SOC 2, HIPAA compliance, data encryption standards, and your incident response process. How do you ensure data isolation in a multi-tenant environment?

Question 4: AI/ML Approach
Explain your approach to AI-powered document understanding and answer generation. What models do you use? How do you handle accuracy and confidence scoring? How do you prevent AI hallucinations?

Question 5: Integration & API
Describe your API capabilities and available integrations. What is your rate limiting policy? Do you provide SDKs? Can your platform integrate with Salesforce and Microsoft Teams?

---

SECTION 3: SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

- Responses should be detailed and specific
- Include relevant case studies or references where possible
- Pricing information should be provided separately
- Technical architecture diagrams are encouraged
