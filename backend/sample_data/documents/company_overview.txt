Company Overview: Iris Technologies

Iris Technologies is a Series B enterprise SaaS company founded in 2020, headquartered in San Francisco, California. We specialize in AI-powered document intelligence and workflow automation for mid-market and enterprise organizations.

Mission: To eliminate repetitive knowledge work so teams can focus on high-value decisions.

Key Facts:
- Founded: 2020
- Employees: 150+
- Customers: 200+ enterprise clients across financial services, healthcare, and government sectors
- Funding: $45M total raised (Series B led by Acme Ventures)
- Annual Revenue: $20M ARR as of 2024

Leadership Team:
- CEO: Sarah Chen (ex-Google, Stanford CS)
- CTO: Marcus Johnson (ex-AWS, MIT)
- VP Engineering: Priya Patel (ex-Stripe)
- VP Sales: David Kim (ex-Salesforce)

Our platform processes over 1 million documents monthly and maintains 99.9% uptime. We serve customers across North America and Europe, with plans to expand to APAC in 2025.

Company values:
1. Customer obsession — every feature ships with customer feedback
2. Technical excellence — we invest in doing things right
3. Transparency — open communication internally and with customers
4. Speed with quality — move fast without breaking things
